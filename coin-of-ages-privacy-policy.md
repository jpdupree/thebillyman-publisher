# Privacy Policy — Coin of Ages

**Last updated:** 2026-03-28

---

## 1. Overview

Coin of Ages ("the game", "the app") is a single-player idle game developed as an independent title. This policy explains what data the game does and does not collect.

**Short version:** Coin of Ages collects no personal data. All game progress is stored locally on your device. We do not use analytics, advertising networks, or any third-party tracking services.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

**We collect no personal data.**

Coin of Ages does not collect, transmit, or share:

- Your name, email address, or contact information
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Location data
- Usage analytics or telemetry
- Purchase history (the game has no in-app purchases)
- Any other personally identifiable information

---

## 3. Local Save Data

Coin of Ages saves your game progress locally on your device. This save file contains:

- Your current era, currency balance, and upgrade state
- Prestige count and relic collection
- Audio and haptic preferences
- Timestamps used to calculate offline earnings

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server and is not accessible to us.

**To delete your save data:** Use the "Reset Progress" option in the Settings panel within the game, or uninstall the app.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

Coin of Ages does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, social media, or data collection services.

---

## 5. Children

Coin of Ages does not knowingly collect information from children. As the game collects no personal data from any user, no special handling for children is required beyond this statement.

---

## 6. Changes to This Policy

If we add features in future versions that involve data collection (such as optional cloud save or analytics), this policy will be updated and users will be notified via an in-app notice before any new data collection begins.
