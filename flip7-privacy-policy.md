# Privacy Policy — Score Tracker for Flip 7

**Last updated:** 2026-04-09

---

## 1. Overview

Score Tracker for Flip 7 ("the app") is a score-keeping utility for the Flip 7 card game, developed as an independent title. This policy explains what data the app does and does not collect.

**Short version:** Score Tracker for Flip 7 collects no personal data. All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not use analytics, advertising networks, or any third-party tracking services.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

**We collect no personal data.**

Score Tracker for Flip 7 does not collect, transmit, or share:

- Your name, email address, or contact information
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Location data
- Usage analytics or telemetry
- Purchase history (the app has no in-app purchases)
- Any other personally identifiable information

---

## 3. Local Game Data

The app stores game data locally on your device. This data includes:

- Player names entered by you for the current game session
- Round scores and game results
- Game settings (target score)

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server and is not accessible to us. Game data is cleared when you start a new game or uninstall the app.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

Score Tracker for Flip 7 does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, social media, or data collection services.

---

## 5. Children

Score Tracker for Flip 7 does not knowingly collect information from children. As the app collects no personal data from any user, no special handling for children is required beyond this statement.

---

## 6. Changes to This Policy

If we add features in future versions that involve data collection (such as optional cloud save or analytics), this policy will be updated and users will be notified via an in-app notice before any new data collection begins.
